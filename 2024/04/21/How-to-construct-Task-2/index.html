How to construct Task 2

How to construct Task 2

  • Introduction - 2 sentences 5min
  • Introduce the topic
  • give a general answer

four commons proposition methods(四种常见的出题方式)

  • Discussion

  • Opinion

  • Problem& Solution

  • Two-part question

1. Discussion

e.g. Some people think that it is more effective for students to study in groups, while others believe that it is better for them to study alone.

==Discuss both views and give your opinion.==

Plan: Topic - study in group or alone; Answer - Some times better alone, usually better in group

(==Topic==)People have different views about the effectiveness of group study as opposed to working alone.

(==Answer二选一==)While there are some benefits to study independently, I believe that group work is usually more ==productive(有益的)==

2. Opinion

e.g. Some people believe that unpaid community service should be a compulsory(强制) part of high school programmers.

==To what extend do you agree and disagree?==

Plan: Topic - community services for all teenagers; Answer - 3 choices： agree, disagree or balanced opinion.

It’s sometime argued that high school students should be made to do some work in their local communities. (==disagree==)**In my opinion, **it would be wrong to force teenagers to do any kind of unsalaried work.

3. Problem& Solution

e.g. Many criminals reoffend after they have been punished. Why do some people continue to commit crimes after they have been punished, and what measure can be taken to tackle this problem?

Plan: Topic - criminals reoffend; Answer - several reasons, a variety of measures(government/community/personal)

(==Topic==)It is true that punishment do not alway deter criminals from committing more crimes.

(==Answer==)There are various reasons why offenders repeatedly break the law, but government should certainly tack steps to address this issue.

4. Two-part question(while - Answer)

e.g. As most people spend a major part of their adult life at work, job satisfaction is an important element individual well-being. What factors contribute to job satisfied?

How realistic is the expectation of job satisfaction for all workers?

plan: Topic - job satisfaction ; Answer - several factor, unrealistic/impossible.

Work plays a central role in our lives(major part of their adult life), and we should all like to ==fulfilled professionally职业成就感/job satisfied==

While a variety of factors may lead to job satisfaction, it would be unrealistic(不现实的) to expect everyone to be happy at work.

Task_1 lesson05(Table)

Pie chart

Table

Table can show

  • any kind of number

  • comparisons

  • changes over time(increase/decrease)

  • a lot of information(maybe too much!!)

    So, we should select, describe and compare the key numbers.

Introduction: 改写

e.g. The table below shoes statistics about the to five countries for international tourism in 2012 and 2013.

改写为:The table compares ==the five highest ranking== countries in terms of(就…而言) the number of ==visits and spent== by tourists over a period of 2 years.

Overview 2 sentences, 2 main points

(==分别列出tourism和revenue两点的最高值==)It’s clear that France was the world’s most popular tourists destination in the years 2012 and 2013. However, the USA earned by far the most revenue(收入，替代money) from tourism over the same period.

Details_1

In 2012, (==Top==)83 million tourisms visited France, and the USA was ==the second most== visited country, with 66.7 million tourism(with后面跟补充的数据). Spain and China each received just under 58 million tourism, while Italy ==was ranked the fifth== with 46.4 million tourist(while后面跟并列的数据). 2013 saw a rise of between 1 and 4 million tourisms visits to each country, with the exception of China, which received 2 million fewer visitors than in the previous years.

两个细节段落，一个分析tourism的数量一个分析revenue的数量

先列举start的位置，然后将上身趋势的国家放一块，再将下降趋势的国家拿出来写一句

一共4句话

Details_2

(==Top==)Spending by tourisms visiting the USA increased from 126.2 billion in 2012 to 139.6 billions in 2013, (and these figures were well over twice as high as those for any other country.) Spain received ==the second highest== amount of tourism revenue rising from 53.6 billion to 56.1 billion, followed by France, China, Italy. ==Interestingly, despite falling number of tourism, China revenue from tourism rose by 1.7 billion in 2013.==

Key skill: selecting information

  • earned by far the most 到目前为止最多……
  • revenue from tourism 从游客中获得的收入
  • ranked the fifth 排名第五
  • 2013 saw a rise of …
  • these figures were well over twice as high as those for any other country 比其他的所有国家都多两倍以上
  • despite falling number of xx, xx rose by 100 million 尽管xx的数量在下降，但是他的xx数据在上升
  • saw a rise, increased, rising from, rose by.

Detail 2 中，第一句话用动名词做主语，列举了第一的国家，然后进行一个宏观的对比，和剩下的四个国家进行一个倍数上的对比。

第二句话把排名第二的Spain的数据列举出来，并且写了一个趋势。

完成趋势之后，受限于篇幅的限制，使用followed加剩下的国家的形式，描写剩下的国家，但是不带具体的数据和年份信息。

最后一句话，列举一个特殊现象，尽管游客的数量在降，但是收入却在增。
